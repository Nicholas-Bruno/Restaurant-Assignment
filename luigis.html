Restaurant Description
Luigi's Pizzeria is a long running family restaurant with a taste for fantastic pizza. Established in 1985, our recipe for the perfect sauce and dough have set us apart from every other pizza spot. We are proud to be serving the people of the Mushroom Kingdom the best pizza around for decades. Voted #1 for best pepperoni pizza by Toad Times magazine for 10 years and running, we have been at the top of pizza making.
